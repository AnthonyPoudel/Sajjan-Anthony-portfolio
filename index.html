The project involves the development of a watch using C++, Arduino, an ultrasonic sensor, a buzzer, and a GPS tracker. The watch is designed to provide users with real-time information about their surroundings, utilizing a range of sensors and functionalities and uses the buzzer to beep when the ultrasonic sensor detects obstacles within a certain distance.
